- Operating as One

One HR is a network of UN organizations working together to offer advisory services for cost effective and risk-informed acquisition and management of talent across the UN System.

Talent Management

OneHR provides organization design, job classification and reference checking services.

Global Advisory Services

OneHR provides advisory recommendations to participating organizations. All administrative decisions are taken by the organization, within its existing Accountability Framework.

Global Network

OneHR is a virtual network of specialized HR professionals providing these services under a common operational umbrella and has an administrative hub in Bonn, Germany through its host organization UNDP/UNV.

Co-Designed Platform

The OneHR operating model is the effort of over 15 UN organizations who continue to contribute to the development of this innovative business model through the sponsorship of the CEB HR Network Co-Chair.

IT Enabled

OneHR is the product of a collaboration between IT leaders and business process owners since its inception.

The current expected annual volume is 3,519 for Job Classification (JC) and 2,600 for Reference Verification (RV). With the operationalization of consultant pools to support the increase in service volume, OneHR is well positioned to keep pace with projected increases. Testimonials from our partners

"At UNDP, we believe that working as joined up UN entities is essential to achieve the 2030 sustainable development goals. UNDP is a decentralized organization operating in 165 developing countries and over 500 office locations. We recruit expertise from around the world and are proud of our multi lingual international workforce. In 2017 we screened over 300,000 job applications, of which 90% were for positions in the field. Considering these volumes and the extent of our distributed operations, OneHR is essential for our success. Not only does OneHR allow us to standardize and reduce duplication of work, it is also an opportunity for UNDP to contribute to UN Management Reform as outlined by the Secretary-General. With a focus on professionalization and support from a state-of-the-art IT system, we consider OneHR well positioned to lead this transformation. We are proud to have OHR pioneering this cutting edge inter-agency effort, and hope to set an example which others can follow."

Susan McDade

Assistant Administrator and Director of the Bureau for Management Services
United Nations Development Programme
